## Observed high-signal ingredients

Repeated patterns in strong prompts:
- clear output format
- explicit composition
- concrete lighting description
- texture/material realism
- readable text called out directly
- panel/layout instructions when relevant
- limited, controlled palette
- one strong focal subject
